How to Sleep?

It's a silly question, but almost everyone doesn't do it.
The minimum you should sleep is 6 hours, but you really need 8 for gains (or higher).
If you don't have enough time to sleep, you're probably lying to yourself or you need to do less stuff.
Not only does more sleep give gains, but it also just supports your life in general.

How to Eat?

Depends on what your goals are:

  • If you want to lose weight (cut down), eat below maintenance level by 300 calories.
  • If you want to maintain weight, eat about your maintenance calories.
  • If you want to gain weight (bulk up), eat above your maintenance level by 300 calories.

How to Train?

You should research this yourself, but here's one of the most popular ways to work out:
PPL (Push Pull Legs) is a 7-day program split that determines what body part you exercise on a specific day. For example:

  • Monday: Push (chest, shoulders, and triceps)
  • Tuesday: Pull (back and biceps)
  • Wednesday: Legs
  • Thursday: Rest
  • Friday: Push
  • Saturday: Pull
  • Sunday: Legs
